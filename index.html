Our Story

Clare grew up in Sacramento, and Jared in Torrance. Clare and Jared met in Santa Barbara at Westmont College in their freshmen year, 2013. Clare’s first memory of Jared was him being the “boombox guy” bringing music to a beach day. Jared’s first memory of Clare was her eating all his Habit sweet potato fries after only asking for “1”… Clare and Jared fell in love and decided to stay in Los Angeles close to family and friends. She’s working as a Family Medicine resident physician at Loma Linda, and he’s a software engineer with Broadridge. They were engaged Christmas Day 2022, and are so excited to be getting married!
